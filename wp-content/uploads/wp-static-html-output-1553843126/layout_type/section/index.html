CAREERS

MECHANICAL ENGINEER

Position Description:

  • Plan and design HVAC, plumbing and fire protection systems for commercial projects
  • Experience with electrical system design, including medium and low voltage distribution systems, communication systems, equipment selection, and short circuit and coordination analytical calculations is required.
  • Consult with clients to determine the scope of services required of a project
  • Conduct field surveys, analysis and selection of systems, evaluating project budgets
  • Prepare design documents and technical specifications
  • Coordinate work with team members, and participating in training for advanced skills development.

Qualifications:

A BSME is required and a PE is preferred with 5-10 years of hands-on design experience in HVAC and Plumbing. The ability to  work multiple projects concurrently is required. Strong people skills are a must.

ELECTRICAL ENGINEER

Position Description:

  • Electrical system design for lighting, power, and communications systems
  • Experience with electrical system design, including medium and low voltage distribution systems, communication systems, equipment selection, and short circuit and coordination analytical calculations is required.
  • Preparation and review of complex calculations such as short circuit and lighting (point to point) utilizing SKM Dapper/Captor and Lithonia Visual programs
  • Preparation of drawings using AutoCAD
  • Preparation and review of equipment and material specifications
  • Project schedule development

Qualifications:

A BSEE degree with at least 5 years of electrical engineering experience designing power and lighting system.
